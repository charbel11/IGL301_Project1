About


Fast Auto Services & Transport S.A.L is an automotive service and towing company. The professionalism and experience of its personnel make it a leading company in the market. Our offices and auto workshop are located at Nahr el Mot industrial zone.

F.A.S.T has many partners, most of which are local insurance companies. Offering a direct auto repair facility and an around-the-clock towing service, we strive to maintain an excellent reputation and customer satisfaction.
